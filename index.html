A few features of this app are that you can't move to the next question without selecting an answer, you may go back and change your answer, the score color changes based on the percentage of your score, and you may press the "c" key if you wish to see the correct answer of the current question, you can use the right and left arrows to navigate forwards and backwards in the quiz as well.
